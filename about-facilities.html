About our Facilities

The Percy Centre boasts a wide range of versatile spaces for private and commercial hire. The centre plays host to a huge variety of community resources for personal development and extracurricular activities for all ages - youth projects, music, dance, holiday play groups - and much more besides! Each of our spaces can be tailored to suit your needs, and are made accessible to everyone. To make choosing the right space for your activity or event easier, we've provided the specifications for all of our rooms as well as additional features and accessability information below.

Reception

Description

Our reception area is wheelchair accessible, and our friendly staff will will do their utmost to make your visit to the centre a stress free and enjoyable experience. There is access through the main entrance via the stairs or ramp and both sets of doors are automatic. From the foyer there is access to the lift, which can accommodate two people (albeit at a bit of a squeeze) which can take you to the other floors. To the left are our ground floor spaces, and the toilets.

Our staff are non-judgemental and take pride in offering equal treatment of everyone in our community regardless of race, gender identity, sexual orientation, or disability. We excersize a zero-tolerance policy towards any kind of bullying or abuse, and will respond accordingly.

Conference Rooms

Floor: Ground Floor
Size: 40ft x 18ft
Commercial Rate: £23 per hour
Community Rate: £15 per hour

Description

The Percy Centre has a number of rooms suitable for meetings, conferences and training sessions. Our rooms can accommodate up to 60 people (theatre style), or up to 30 people (boardroom style). We have a range of presentation resources availablle alongside hi-speed WIFI, refreshments and catering options.

Actvity Room

Floor: Ground Floor
Size: 40ft x 18ft
Commercial Rate: £23 per hour
Community Rate: £15 per hour

Description

A versatile room with both carpeted and vinyl floor areas, ideal for children's activities. It also lends itself well to creative arts and crafts, as well as informal meetings. The room can be divided into two smaller spaces, and has a kitchen and food serving area.
