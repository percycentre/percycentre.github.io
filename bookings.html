Room Bookings

Get in touch today

Percy Community Centre is the ideal venue for meetings, conferences, training, sports, parties and much more. Our wide variety of available spaces allow us to cater for almost any type of event. We have a full selection of presentation equipment and on site technical support to ensure everything runs smoothly. We also have our own well equipped computer suites with interactive white board and multimedia facilities. All our rooms have WIFI access with high speed fibre optic broadband.
