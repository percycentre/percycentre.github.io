Percy Community Centre

A vibrant community centre in the heart of Bath

Who we are

We are a charity run by the local community and supported by local businesses to provide spaces for community projects in the Bath area.

The centre plays host to a wide variety of community resources for personal development and extracurricular activities for all ages - youth projects, music, dance, holiday play groups - and much more besides!

About our Staff

What we do

The percy centre is a community and commercial venue for hire, with a variety of rooms and spaces available to suit your needs.

The premises is composed of a reception and waiting room as well as seven spaces each of which is specialised to suit a wide range of activities.
